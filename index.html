OUR STORY

Our team comes with the experience and knowledge

As per a UNICEF report, around 90% of students - while going through theoretical learning (which includes virtual and classroom learning), achieve a maximum of 50% of subject grasp - and depend on rote learning for the rest, thus significantly amplifying the exam-oriented studying mindset.

To overcome this scenario and help students better their grasp, we (a group of Scientists, Engineers & Teachers) envisioned the Hands-On Science™ (HOS) kits - filled with carefully designed DIY experiments to address the curricular requirements of all major education boards in india, across grades 1 to 12.
